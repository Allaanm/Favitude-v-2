Documentation

Favitude is your one-stop destination for favicon generation. Create custom, professional favicons from text, images, or emojis in minutes.

Our platform handles all the technical details, generating widely compatible `.ico` and `.png` formats along with the necessary HTML code to make your site look awesome on every browser and device.

Features

Generate from Text

No logo? No problem. Create a brand identity instantly by generating unique favicons from your brand's initials or name.

Generate from Image

Already have a logo? Upload it and we'll convert it into a perfectly optimized favicon package for all platforms.

Save to Drafts

Not ready to download? Save your designs to your drafts and come back to tweak them later.

Browse Collection

Need inspiration? Browse our extensive library of pre-generated favicons ready for immediate use.

How to Generate

Image Generator

Perfect for when you already have a brand asset.

1
Click "Generate" from the home page or navbar.
2
Select the "Upload Image" option.
3
Upload your image file (PNG, JPG, or SVG).
4
Click "Generate Favicon" to download your zip file.

Text Generator

Create a unique icon from scratch using typography.

1
Navigate to the "Generate" page.
2
Select "Convert Texts".
3
Customize your text, font, size, and colors.
4
Preview your design and click "Generate".

Usage & Integration

Once you have downloaded your `favicon.ico` file, here is how to add it to your website:

  1. Rename your file to favicon.ico (if it isn't already).
  2. Upload the file to the root directory of your website (e.g., yourwebsite.com/favicon.ico).
  3. Browsers will automatically detect it, but for best results, link it in your HTML <head>.
